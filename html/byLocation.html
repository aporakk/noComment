Classic Literature emerges in Europe

The term reffered to “classic” is used for the literature of any language in a period known for its greatness and permanent quality of the work of the writers. Classical Literature emerges in ancient Greece in the period that was expanded from about 500 to 320 years before the new era. As for the classic literature in Rome, the period between 70 BCE and 18 CE was known as The Golden Age.
